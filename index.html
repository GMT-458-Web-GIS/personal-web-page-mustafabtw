About Me

My journey began in Bandırma on December 24, 2003, continued through primary and secondary education in Ankara, and led me to Kurtuluş Anatolian High School. Currently, I am channeling my passion for geospatial data science into an academic career at Hacettepe University's Department of Geomatics Engineering. Throughout my education, I have gained practical experience with modern technologies like Python, QGIS, and AutoCAD Civil3D via internships and my graduation project. My goal is to leverage these skills to contribute to innovative projects and specialize in my field.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I cultivate discipline and personal growth through sports such as Basketball, Jiu-Jitsu, and Kickboxing. Cinema and amateur photography are also among my greatest passions, allowing me to see the world through a different lens. My career objective is to take this versatile perspective to an international platform and, after graduation, build a balanced and serene life abroad where I can both practice my profession passionately and dedicate time to my personal interests.
